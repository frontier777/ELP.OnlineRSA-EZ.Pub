Workplace Hygiene for Food Handlers (non accredited)

Overview

This course gives hospitality staff the necessary skills in food safety and food hygiene matters, ensuring compliance with the Australia New Zealand Food Standards Code Standard 3.2.2 Food Safety Practices and General Requirements.
This course is divided into the following four sections each with an online assessment;

  1. Introduction
  2. You and your workplace
  3. Understanding contamination
  4. Typical workplace tasks

Online Course Duration

The course is 100% online with an assessment at the end of each section and takes about 1.5-2 hrs to complete.

Learning Outcomes

This course provided the following learning outcomes:

  1. Understand the obligation of food handlers under current legislation
  2. Maintain the correct standards of personal hygiene
  3. The correct methods of food handling and storage
  4. Hygiene in relation to typical workplace tasks
  5. Identify potential hygiene risks and how to prevent them
  6. The correct methods for cleaning and sanitation
  7. The importance of pest and waste management
  8. Allergen awareness
  9. Understanding of contamination
