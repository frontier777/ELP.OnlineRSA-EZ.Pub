State by State Requirements

Can I get one RSA training course that will cover the whole of Australia?

Yes, but..

Unfortuntely some states and territories have differing requirements for RSA training, assessment and certificate/badge/competency cards. Therefore it is not possible to do one single course that will satisfy all requirements for all states, but, you are able to bridge your qualification to the other states.

Our recommendation is that you should choose the state where you are most likely to start working and complete that course, making sure to click on all of the available state variations when you come to the map sections. Once you have your Provide Responsible Service of Alcohol SITHFAB021 statement of attainment, it will be recognised in TAS, WA, NT, SA, ACT, & QLD. If you did not choose NSW or VIC as your first choice, you can then do the free online bridging courses for those states, if and when you need to work there.

The VIC government's bridging course is currently free. The NSW government ultimately levies an additional fee for their photo competency card scheme and at the time of writing this fee is set at $97.

  • NSW online bridging course - free, but please note you will still need to pay for your NSW competency card.
  • VIC online bridging course - free.

See each states course page for full details, or continue reading below.

What is required to work in the liquor Industry in Queensland (QLD)?

The Nationally Accredited Provide Responsible Service of Alcohol SITHFAB021 (RSA) online training course that we offer is the most recent accreditation for RSA training and is accepted in Queensland.

Our online course has specific content for Queensland, such as laws and fines specific to Queensland. The nationally recognised certificate issued for this course is valid in QLD, WA, SA, NT, TAS, ACT and is also valid for the Airlines. What is required to work in the liquor Industry in New South Wales (NSW)?

You must complete a training course delivered by a Liquor and Gaming NSW Approved Training Provider (ATP) either online or in the classroom. We are an approved provider, you should look for the NSW approved provider logo on the NSW state page.

Please note: RSA "correspondence" training is no longer permitted in NSW.

Our online course has specific content for New South Wales, such as laws and fines specific to New South Wales. On completion of the course you will receive 2 certificates. A NSW Interim RSA Certificate and a nationally recognised Provide Responsible Service of Alcohol SITHFAB021 (RSA) certificate.

The nationally recognised certificate issued for this course is valid in QLD, WA, SA, NT, TAS, ACT and is also valid for the Airlines. If at a later time you wish to work in VIC, there is a free online bridging courses available.

To work in NSW you must have your "NSW interim RSA certificate" or a "NSW Photo Competency Card", that you will apply for with your "NSW interim RSA certificate".

The interim certificate allows you to work for up to 90 days, after which time you must have applied for and received your photo competency card.

If your interim certificate expires before you have your photo competency card you will not be able to work, so make sure you apply for your competency card as soon as possible.

NSW RSA Photo Competency Card Renewals / Refresher Course

If you already hold a NSW Photo Competency Card for Responsible Service of Alcohol, you must not do the RSA training again. Instead you must complete the NSW government's online refresher course operated by the government and available via the Liquor and Gaming NSW website.

At the time of writing, the cost of the RSA referesher course is $35.
