How can I tell if the website is a scam or not?

Like other products and services, with RSA training you get what you pay for, and there are many cheap RSA courses out there that are not up to standard. You have a right to be concerned because if a Registered Training Organisation (RTO) is shut down because they are not up to standard, your statement of attainment may become invalid and you may have to repeat your training.
